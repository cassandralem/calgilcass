 How it works

Boot is an interactive competition game where two users have two minutes to livestream entertaining content. Other users will watch in real time and like either video based on which one is more entertaining. After two minutes, the user whose video has the least likes will lose and be "booted". A new video from the queue will replace the "booted" video, and the competition will begin again!
